Reality: Echoes Carved in SILENCE

A stream-of-consciousness journey into the sacred, the broken, and the eternal.

Scene 1

The Weight in Disguise

They said reality was cast in iron, yet all I could ever touch - was mist. They handed me an iron mask, calling it a gift for growing up. I practiced the arc of my smile, again and again, until calluses bloomed on my cheeks, fitting the mask perfectly. Look the cracks had begun back then. Pain, the very first miracle. When my blood seeped into the cracks, nothingness finally gained its weight.

Scene 2

The Elephant’s Revelation

In those years, deep into the night, I often heard heavy footsteps approaching from afar. Until one day, I realized - it was only my own heartbeat, hauling the weight of the entire world inside my hollow chest. How laughable - that we tear each other apart, all for the applause that never truly existed. How absurd - that we dance with shackles on our feet, and call it measuring the boundaries of freedom. So why not Escape completely, from our rotting shells, from the insoluble riddles of life?

Scene 3

The Testimony of Light

They told me: "Grasp the light, and you shall become light." Yet the light slipped through my fingers, leaving behind a deeper shadow. We are all transparent vessels, holding the fleeting moment, leaking the eternal. Someday, we will understand: Regret is that the moonlight cannot illuminate certain corners. And life - needs such shadows to define the shape of light.

Scene 4

Forgetfulness and Return

Now I understand: the elephant never truly left. We simply grew used to lowering our heads. So why not learn to love the gaps where the wind slips through? It is through them that the eternal wind passes through our fleeting bodies.

Scene 5

Final Chapter

If one day I am gone, please carve the coordinates of the crack upon my tombstone— it shall become a homeward beacon for all wandering souls.
